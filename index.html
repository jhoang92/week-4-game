Crystal Collector

Welcome to the Crystal Collector! The game will start you off with a number, you will press on one of the four crystals to obtain points. When you match your score to the random number chosen, you win!

If you go over the total score given, you will lose!

The crystal values are unknown until you press them and will randomize each game.
